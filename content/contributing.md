---
title: "Contributing to FAST-HEP"
---

FAST-HEP is developed openly, and contributions of all sizes are welcome.

There are many ways to contribute, including:

* reporting bugs or suggesting improvements
* improving documentation
* adding tutorials and examples
* writing tests
* contributing analysis operations or other capabilities
* improving infrastructure and developer tooling
* working on the FAST-HEP packages themselves

The contribution guidelines are maintained centrally in the `fasthep` repository:

[**Read the FAST-HEP contribution guide →**](https://github.com/FAST-HEP/fasthep/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)

The guide covers the development environment, coding and testing conventions, documentation, larger architectural changes, and AI-assisted contributions.

## Questions and ideas

You do not need to have an implementation ready before getting involved.

For questions, ideas, or project-wide discussions, use [FAST-HEP Discussions](https://github.com/FAST-HEP/fasthep/discussions).

For concrete bugs and feature requests, use the [FAST-HEP issue tracker](https://github.com/FAST-HEP/fasthep/issues).
