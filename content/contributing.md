# Contributing to FAST-HEP

FAST-HEP is developed openly, and contributions to code, documentation, examples, testing, and infrastructure are welcome.

The contribution guidelines are maintained centrally in the `fasthep` repository:

[**FAST-HEP contribution guide →**](https://github.com/FAST-HEP/fasthep/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)

For questions and project-wide discussions, see the [FAST-HEP Discussions](https://github.com/FAST-HEP/fasthep/discussions).
